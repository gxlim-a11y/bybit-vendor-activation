What Bybit is Looking For
Review campaign goals before proceeding
Preferred
✅ eFTT Buy
Users deposit ≥ $100 (eFTD) and make their first trade (eFTT). This is Bybit's primary revenue event — trading fees are only earned when users trade. Agreeing to eFTT will fast-track your onboarding.
Conditional
⚠️ eFTD Buy
Deposit ≥ $100 within 14 days of sign-up. Accepted with additional TnC agreement. Note: eFTD alone generates no revenue for Bybit until the user goes on to trade.
Not Recommended
❌ CPM / CPC / CPI / Signup
Based on historical data these buy types do not perform to Bybit's ROI expectations and are not recommended. Campaigns under these models are unlikely to be approved.
Bybit's core principle: We evaluate ROI on every vendor investment. If eFTDs are not trading, or trading below payout levels, that traffic is classified as less valid or invalid. We are exclusively targeting high-quality users who deposit and trade.

3
Key Points for Confirmation
All points must be acknowledged before onboarding
🔐
PID Requirement — Macro String
Before launching any campaign, a macro string must be appended to the PID (VTA/CTA). Bybit will provide standardized template links for you to apply the macro directly.

⚠️ Any PID running links not aligned with our required format will result in eFTDs not being billable.
🛡️
Fraud Policy
Our internal risk team flags fraudulent users based on abnormal behaviours at the eFTD level, including but not limited to:
  • Use of externally purchased KYC services
  • Multiple fake or emulated devices abusing reward mechanisms
Such users will be classified as invalid and excluded from billing. A consolidated fraud report is shared at the end of each month and applies to all vendors.
🚀
Campaign Briefing & Launch Flow
Campaign assets (budget, creatives, tracking links) will be shared via email. Do not launch any campaign without prior written confirmation from Bybit.

All tracking links must be adjusted to match our internal attribution requirements. Do not run unmodified links or remove existing parameters.

Non-compliance may result in eFTDs being deemed invalid and non-billable.
🌍
Restricted Markets
Advertising is not permitted in the following countries:
Singapore Malaysia Thailand Philippines Canada New Zealand France United States Uzbekistan United Kingdom Japan
💳
Billing Validation
Bybit validates and pays only for eFTDs reflected in the raw report that meet both conditions:
  • IS Primary Attribution = TRUE
  • Retargeting = NO
📊
Attribution Rules
Signup: First-touch attribution
eFTD: Last-click attribution

Once a user signs up under your account or campaign ID, the signup remains attributed to you. Other vendors cannot hijack or redirect this data to claim the eFTD.
